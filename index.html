How “Download audio” works

  1. Click Download audio below.
  2. A browser prompt will pop up asking what to share — choose “This tab”.
  3. Turn on “Share tab audio” before confirming (it's usually a toggle at the bottom-left of that prompt).
  4. Your text starts speaking automatically — just wait for it to finish.
  5. The recording stops itself and the audio file downloads on its own.

Works best in Chrome or Edge on desktop. No sign-up, no API key — everything happens on your device.

Runs entirely in your browser — nothing you say or type leaves this page.
